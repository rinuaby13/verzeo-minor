Spark 2K21

Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science


      Sports Day

brings new energy in the hearts and minds of the students. This day is marked with great joy and discovering the inner talent of students in the field of sports. A sound mind resides in a sound body. Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science proudly presents,Spark 2021 which will be conducted on 17th July 2021. Our chief guest will be Mr I.M Vijayan,former professional Indian footbal player.Spark is conducted every year to bring out the talent in individuals and to bring the sports and man spirit in all.MITS have well trained basketball, volleyball team and have bagged a lot of prizes for many inter college sports event. The major highlights of the day are march past,aerobics,relay and tug of war.So lets join our hand and make this event a grand success.
